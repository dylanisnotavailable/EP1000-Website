Final Project

What does it do?

This is a simple lamp that emits a soothing blue light. The initial idea was for it to respond to sound, as well as play a few select sound bytes. However, this has fallen by the wayside as shipments for crucial components was delayed.

I opted to place a clear cover over the electronic compnents, temprarily held on by tape but a more permanent solution will be added soon.

I fully intend on adding the initially-planned features when I am able to, in order to be fully satisfied with this project.
